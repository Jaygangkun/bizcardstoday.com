About Boone Creek Creamery

Deep in the heart of Kentucky’s Bluegrass region, in one of Lexington’s urban industrial areas, sits the headquarters for Ed Puterbaugh’s Boone Creek Creamery. Boone Creek Creamery creates handmade artisan cheeses such as Abbey Road, Jerk Cheddar, Kentucky Derby, Mama Mia, Sassy Redhead and Wildcat Blue using Old World European techniques to protect the delicate curds. We believe that hand making our artisan cheese creates a richer, more complex flavor. We also only use the best Kentucky Proud milk from cows in south eastern Kentucky that graze on nutrient rich grasses as nature intended—free of antibiotics, hormones and steroids. Come in for a tour and a taste of our cheese making facility. You’ll taste for yourself why Boone Creek Creamery is howlin’ good.
